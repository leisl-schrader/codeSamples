Let's Jam®
Extra Hold Gel

Spikey Layers

  1. Start with a short asymmetrical layered cut
  2. Spray in a leave-in conditioning treatment and blow dry or wrap hair.
  3. Once dry apply a hair cream to hair and flat iron hair under and front hairline, flat iron hair forward onto face.
  4. In the crown flat iron hair straight up with just a slight bend at ends.
  5. In hands emulsify a nickel-sized amount of LETS JAM EXTRA HOLD and placing fingers in at roots and tousle hair to separate.
  6. With LETS JAM EXTRA HOLD just on fingertips separate crown and hairline to give definition to finish.

Let's Jam®
Regular Hold

Pompadour

  1. Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Spray in a leave-in conditioning treatment and blow dry your hair forward and then backwards with paddle brush. (This will allow your hair to move in either direction when curled.)
  3. When your hair is completely dry, apply a dime-sized amount of hair cream. Then, make a diagonal part starting behind your ear and then with a flat iron, iron the sides down smooth, working towards the front of your hairline.
  4. Then, using a medium sized barrel iron, start at your crown and curl your hair back towards nape.
  5. When you've reached the very top of your head, comb each section of hair slightly forward and keep directing your curls back. (This will start to create a looser curl.) As you reach the front of your hairline, use a larger iron to add a bit of a bump to your hair.
  6. Apply a pea-sized amount of LETS JAM REGULAR HOLD to hands and then finger comb your hair to separate the top curls and to smooth the front of your hairline back.
  7. Then apply a nickel-sized amount of LETS JAM REGULAR HOLD to your hands. Rub them together to emulsify the gel and then apply to smooth down the sides and back of your hair.
  8. Lightly backcomb the front of your hairline to give your Pompadour volume in front and then comb it into shape. Finish by spraying your entire head with your favorite holding spray to lock your style in place.

    (If your hair is longer on the sides, use bobby pins to hold your hair back after sleeking with LETS JAM.)

Custard

Ponytail

  1. Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioning treatment and blow dry your hair smooth.
  3. Apply small amount of hair cream to your hair and comb it through to distribute evenly.
  4. Take small section and flat iron hair to make it sleek and shiny while bumping your ends under.
  5. Part off an off-center triangular section of hair to create a side swept bang.
  6. Using a small-tooth comb, lightly backcomb the hair in your crown for added volume.
  7. Apply a small amount of LETS JAM CUSTARD to your hands and rub them together to emulsify the product, and then smooth your hair up into a center ponytail and secure.
  8. If your sides have any fly-aways, use a small amount of LETS JAM CUSTARD to smooth.

TAGS:

African American Hair, Smooth, Sophisticated, Long Hair, Classic, Simple, Contemporary, Casual, Black Hairstyles

Let's Jam®
Lite Hold Gel

Short Natural

  1. Start by doing a co-poo by shampooing natural hair with conditioner only.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a pick attachment or comb to elongate your curl.
  3. Then take random large sections of hair and apply a small amount of LETS JAM LITE HOLD and comb thru each section.
  4. Twist sections of your hair together and then let it sit for at least one hour (or sit under a hooded dryer).
  5. Finish by unwrapping your twists and finger style your hair.

African American Hair, Kinky, Short Hair, Natural, Soft, Classic, Wavy, Casual, Simple, Black Hairstyles

The Up-Do

  1. Start with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioning treatment.
  2. Apply a setting lotion and then blow dry your hair.
  3. Then use a small amount of heat protector on your hair and with a flat iron or large iron loosely curl your hair.
  4. Part your hair on the side and sweep your curls off towards the heavy side of the part.
  5. Using a nickel-sized amount of LETS JAM CUSTARD smooth the hair on the shallow side of your part back and around your head. Then secure it into a loose side ponytail with a coated ponytail holder.
  6. To create chignon on the side, divide your side ponytail into three equal sections.
  7. Take the first section and twist it counter clockwise, as if to make a loose pincurl, and then secure it with bobby pinsâ€”leaving your ends free.
  8. Then take the second section and twist it clockwise, as if to make a pincurl, and secure it with bobby pins right beside the previous section, leaving your ends free.
  9. The third section should be twisted counter clockwise again and then laid on top of your previously pinned sections. And then secured with bobby pins to create a chignon.
  10. To finish, apply a dime-sized amount of LETS JAM CUSTARD to your fingertips and separate your loose ends to give definition to your curls.
  11. Work more of the LETS JAM CUSTARD through your fringe area and comb it over towards your chignon for a dramatic side swept bang.

Regular Hold Gel

Short Style

  1. Start with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioning treatment.
  2. Apply a setting lotion and wrap or blow dry your hair.
  3. Once your hair is dry, take small triangle-shaped sections and starting at your crown, spiral curl your hair on top with a flat iron or small barrel curling iron.
  4. The hair on your sides and back should be flat ironed downwards.
  5. Then place pea-sized amount of LETS JAM REGULAR HOLD on your fingertips and separate your spiral curls to add shine and definition.
  6. To finish, apply a nickel-sized amount to your hands and rub them together to emulsify the product. Use this to sleek your sides back and back down to create tapered silhouette.
