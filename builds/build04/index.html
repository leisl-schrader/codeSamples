How ThankYou® Rewards Works

How to Earn Points

It's easy and fast to earn ThankYou Points. You can earn them by doing your everyday shopping with an eligible Citi Card. And you can earn even more by shopping at the ThankYou Bonus Center. Plus, you can get points for selected banking activities.

How to Get Rewards

Redeem your points for millions of rewards in the ThankYou Rewards online catalogue. The possibilities are virtually endless – the catalogue has everything from books to electronics to travel. And at just 10,000 points, you can contact a Wish Specialist and request something unique.

How to Get Started

You can use several eligible Citi Cards to earn points, including Citi ThankYou Cards, Citi Forward Cards and more. Apply for the card with the right benefits for you at citicards.com. You can also earn points through everyday banking services if you have an enrolled Citi checking account that's linked to qualified products and services.
